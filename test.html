Wheat growing areas

Description

Wheat is a cereal grain, which is considered to be a staple food in many countries. It is the third most produced grain in the world after maize and rice. Wheat is used in food production and also as a key ingredient in animal feed. Higher quality wheat is milled into flour and used in products like bread, cakes, biscuits and pasta or reconstituted into breakfast cereals. Low quality wheat is often used in animal feed.

Global production: 745,000,000 MT
